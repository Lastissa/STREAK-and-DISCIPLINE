Under the Hood

How this report is calculated

No black box. Here's exactly where every number above comes from.

  • Completed check-ins divided by days elapsed in the period, weighted slightly toward recent days so a strong finish counts a little more than a strong start. Today only counts once the day has ended in your local timezone.

  • Consecutive days with at least one completed check-in, counting backward from the most recent entry. A single missed day resets the streak to zero — we don't offer streak freezes, since the whole point is an honest count.

  • The single emoji you pick when you check in, nothing inferred from your writing. We never run sentiment analysis on your private notes — mood is only what you explicitly tell us.

  • Plain word count on your entry text, and the "morning vs evening" or "travel risk" insights are simple correlations across your own history — compared only to your past weeks, never to other users.
