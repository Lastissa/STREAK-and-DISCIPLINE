Help Center

How do I start my first commitment?

After signing up, you'll set a commitment, define your "why," and choose a minimum effort. Then check in daily.

How do I create an account?

Click Start Free — sign up with email, Google, or Facebook.

I can't log into my account

Use "Forgot Password" or ensure you're using the same social login method. Contact support if stuck.

How can I delete my data?

Self-service via Account Settings → Delete Account, email, or WhatsApp. See section 8.

Is my data really private?

Yes. Journal entries are private. Leaderboards only show your display name and streak.

Can I export my data?

Yes — JSON or CSV from Account Settings → Export Data.

Do you use tracking cookies?

No. Essential cookies only.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Account Settings → Billing → Cancel. Features stay until period ends.

Can I get a refund?

7‑day money‑back guarantee for first‑time Pro subscribers.

What's the difference between plans?

Starter (free): 3 commitments. Pro: unlimited + AI. Team: adds admin & API.

What happens if I miss a day?

Streak resets (unless you have a Freeze token). The app asks you to reflect honestly.

How do I join an accountability group?

Create a group and invite friends. They see consistency, never your entries.

What are Streak Freeze tokens?

Miss one day without resetting. Earn by maintaining streaks. Capped at 3.

How do I set up reminders?

Account Settings → Reminders. Choose time, method, and frequency.

Can I use the app without leaderboards?

Yes. Leaderboards are optional. Disable in Account Settings → Privacy.
