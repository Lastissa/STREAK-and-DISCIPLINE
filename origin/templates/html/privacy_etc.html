Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

At STREAK & DISCIPLINE ("we," "our," or "us"), your privacy is the foundation of our product. We are not in the business of selling or exploiting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and an optional username when you create an account. We do not require your real name.
  • Journal Content: The commitments you set, daily check‑in answers, reflections, and streaks. This is your private data — we do not access it for any purpose other than to display it to you and generate your weekly summaries.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as pages visited, feature usage, and error logs to help us improve the service. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Optional Data: If you enable reminders, we store your preferred reminder time and method. If you join a leaderboard or accountability group, your display name and streak count may be visible to other participants.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account.
  • To display your journal, streaks, and analytics to you.
  • To send you reminders and weekly analysis emails (if opted in).
  • To improve our product through aggregated usage analytics.
  • To communicate with you about service updates, security alerts, and support inquiries.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3). We implement industry‑standard security measures, including regular vulnerability scans and access controls.

4. Your Rights

  • Access & Portability: You can export all your journal data from your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data permanently.
  • Correction: You can edit or delete individual journal entries anytime.
  • Opt‑out: You can disable reminders, leaderboards, and any optional features from your settings.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (session) and to remember your theme preference. We do not use tracking cookies or third‑party advertising cookies.

6. Third‑Party Services

We use trusted third‑party services for email delivery, hosting, and analytics. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 10.

Frequently asked questions and guides

How do I start my first commitment?

After signing up, you'll be guided through onboarding. Set a commitment, define your "why," and choose a minimum effort that's so small you can't skip it. Then check in daily.

See How It Works →

How do I create an account?

Click Start Free on any page. You can sign up with your email and password, or use Google or Facebook for one-click registration.

Create Account →

I can't log into my account

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you signed up with Google or Facebook, make sure you're using the same method. Still stuck? Contact support.

How do I change my username?

Go to Account Settings → Profile. You can update your username anytime. This is the name shown on leaderboards and groups.

How can I delete my data?

You have three options: Self-service via Account Settings → Delete Account, Email us at support@streakanddiscipline.com, or WhatsApp us. Full details in our Privacy Policy section 9.

See Data Deletion Policy →

Is my data really private?

Yes. Your journal entries are private by default. No one — not even our staff — can read them. Leaderboards only show your display name and streak count. Read our full privacy policy.

Privacy Policy →

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export all your journal entries, streaks, and analytics as JSON or CSV from Account Settings → Export Data.

Do you use tracking cookies?

No. We only use essential cookies for authentication and theme preferences. We do not use tracking, advertising, or third-party cookies. See section 5 of our Privacy Policy.

You have the right to access, export, correct, and delete your data at any time. You can also opt out of optional features like reminders and leaderboards. See section 4 of our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Go to Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the billing period. No questions asked.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time Pro subscribers. Contact support to request a refund. See section 6 of our Terms.

Refund Policy →

How do I update my payment method?

Navigate to Account Settings → Billing → Payment Methods to add, remove, or update your payment information.

What's the difference between plans?

Starter (free) includes 3 commitments and basic features. Pro adds unlimited commitments, AI insights, and priority support. Team adds admin dashboard and API access. Compare plans.

See Pricing →

What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak resets (unless you have a Streak Freeze token). The app doesn't shame you — it asks you to reflect honestly. The goal is self-awareness, not perfection.

Can I use the app offline?

Currently an internet connection is required. Offline support is planned for future updates.

How do I join an accountability group?

Create a group from the Groups section and invite friends via email or link. Members see check-in consistency, never content.

What are Streak Freeze tokens?

A Streak Freeze lets you miss one day without resetting your streak. Earn tokens by maintaining consistency (e.g., 7-day streak = 1 token). Capped at 3.

Is there a mobile app?

The website is fully responsive. A native mobile app is planned for early 2027.

How do I set up reminders?

Go to Account Settings → Reminders. Choose your preferred time, method (email, push, WhatsApp), and frequency. Learn more.

How do AI insights work?

AI analysis is optional. When enabled, it scans your journal entries for patterns (mood trends, productivity correlations) and surfaces them in your weekly report. Your data is never used to train external models.

Can I use the app without leaderboards?

Absolutely. Leaderboards are completely optional. You can disable them in Account Settings → Privacy and use the app purely as a private journal.
